English Version

1) Who we are

This Privacy Policy explains how our application (“the App”) handles personal data for users in Thailand.

2) What we collect

  • We do not collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, precise location, advertising IDs, or device identifiers.
  • The App works without account registration.
  • All barcode, coupon, and eGift data are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

3) How we use data

  • Since we do not collect personal data, we do not use, share, sell, or disclose your information to third parties.
  • If third-party ads are displayed in your region, they are shown without sharing your personal data with advertisers.

4) Storage & deletion

  • Information remains locally on your device.
  • Deleting the App removes locally stored data.

5) Your rights in Thailand (PDPA)

Under Thailand’s Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA), you may have rights to be informed, access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing, and to complain to competent authorities. As the App does not collect or process your personal data on external systems, these rights are inherently respected. If you have questions, contact us below.

6) Children’s privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7) Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The latest version will be available at this page/URL. Your continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated Policy.

8) Contact

For questions or requests, contact: pointegiftapp@gmail.com

  • เราไม่เก็บข้อมูลส่วนบุคคล เช่น ชื่อ อีเมล หมายเลขโทรศัพท์ ตำแหน่งที่ตั้งที่แม่นยำ รหัสโฆษณา หรือรหัสอุปกรณ์

  • เนื่องจากเราไม่เก็บข้อมูลส่วนบุคคล เราจะไม่ใช้ แบ่งปัน ขาย หรือเปิดเผยข้อมูลของคุณให้กับบุคคลที่สาม
